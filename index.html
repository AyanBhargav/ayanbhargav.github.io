Recipe #1

  • Mutton Curry.
  • Time: 2-3 hours
  • Serves: 6 peoples
Ingredients Quantity
Meat Masala 2 packets
Sliced Mutton 2 Kilogram

Step 1: Buy sliced Mutton.

Step 2: Fry onions.

Step 3: Put fried onions in cooker with mutton.

Step 4: Put salt(4 teaspoons) and masalas.

Step 5: Cook it for 3 hours, place salads (onion rings for me) and serve hot.
